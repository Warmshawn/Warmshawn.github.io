What do etc., aka, ie, and et mean? - Quora

  • a.k.a
    also know as
  • et
    Latin for “and.” You might have seen et al., abbreviation for Latin et alii, “and others.” Sometimes used to refer to a group by mentioning only one member, as with a book that has several authors. “Jones et al.”
  • etc.
    Latin et cetera, “and so forth” in English., meaning and other similar things.It is used to avoid giving a complete list.
  • i.e.
    Latin id est, “that is.” Used to indicate that one thing is the same as another, or that a list is complete. Sometimes confused with “e.g.”, an abbreviation for the Latin exempli gratia, “for example” in English. This indicates that the list is incomplete.
